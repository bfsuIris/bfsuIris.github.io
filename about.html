    选段译文质量比对

    原文：Historically, as well as today, nurses are at the forefront of fighting epidemics and pandemics that threaten health across the globe. (from WHO Report)

    DeepL翻译:无论是在历史上，还是今天，护士们都站在抗击威胁全球健康的流行病和大流行病的前沿。

    Google翻译:从历史上到今天，护士都在与威胁全球健康的流行病和大流行作斗争的最前沿。

    英译汉表意准确、通顺自然

    原文：坚定不移推进改革开放,发展活力和内生动力进一步增强。完善要素市场化配置体制机制。深入推进“放管服”改革,实施优化营商环境条例。（2021政府工作报告）

    DeepL翻译：Firmly promote reform and opening up, development vitality and endogenous power to further enhance. Improve the institutional mechanism of market-oriented allocation of factors. Deeply promote the "management and administration" reform, the implementation of regulations to optimize the business environment.

    有道翻译:We unswervingly pursued reform and opening up, and increased the vitality and internal driving force for development. We will improve the systems and mechanisms for market-based allocation of factors of production. We will deepen reforms to streamline administration, delegate power, and improve services, and implement regulations to improve the business environment.

    汉译英更简洁，但仍需译后编辑
